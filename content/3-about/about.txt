About: 

Astrid Seme is an independent graphic designer from Graz, Austria. She received her MA at Werkplaats Typografie, NL after graduating from University for Applied Arts, Vienna. Her studio works within the versatile fields of graphic design: art direction, visual identities, editorial projects—in web, print and space. In high esteem of contents the studios’ key principles are based on research, visual concepts and economical means of production.

In 2014 she received a residency at WIELS Contemporary Art Centre in Brussels for her design work in conjunction with sound. Besides she’s part of [Mark Pezinger Verlag](http://www.markpezinger.de), a publishing house and exhibition-platform for artists’ books.
