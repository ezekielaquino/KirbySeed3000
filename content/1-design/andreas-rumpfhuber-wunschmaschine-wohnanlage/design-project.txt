Text: In his ‘machine désirante’, architect and theorist Andreas Rumpfhuber creates a spatio-social model of condensed, dynamic living and working for the future city of Vienna. From a haptic perspective the book is an economic paperback that appears less than a theory book but as a generous picture book, which unfolds in the hand of its readers, offering space for new and progressive ways of thinking. The giant stones, set in lilac on the cover, form a dreamlike sculpture of cohabitation.
