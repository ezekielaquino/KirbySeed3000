Text: Sleeping disorders are internationally classified into 88 categories, ranging from “Long Sleep” to “Psycho-physiologic Insomnia”. In this sound piece, a calm male voice recites one after another, based on the method of sheep counting. Acoustically, the meaning changes to a positive one: what robs you of sleep as a symptom, lulls you to sleep through a list of designations.
